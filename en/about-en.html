ABOUT US

Meinolf Mertens

Born in Germany, but I live in Italy since 1985, afterwards a trip in this country that left me literally amazed. I spent the first four years in Rome, working with the Goethe Institut, after that I gradually moved to Perugia where I currently stay, teaching German at the University. Although, with my son we head back to Germany every year for the summer holidays to visit friends and relatives. I’m always interested in approaching new cultures and coming in contact with new environments and people. Recently I’ve been on a trip to Iran, with a local friend who I met several years ago while he was still a student. In Teheran I tried to learn a little bit of Farsi, in addition to that I speak fluently 4 languages: German, Italian, English and French. We also have a dog named Maya, a sweet and meek Golden Retriever, she’s a bit old by now, but always joyful and affectionate.
